  • Here are a few things to consider
    before you reach for that GMO-free tomato.

    GMO (Genetically Modified Organism), also known as a transgenic organism,
    is any organism whose genetic material has been altered
    using genetic engineering techniques. - Wikipedia

1. It's complicated.

GMOs are not evil, nor are they harmless. It's important to know that
the GMO issue is complex. For instance, a GMO label may not tell you that
the apple you're holding was sprayed with dangerous herbicides.
At the same time, you can have an organic, genetically engineered apple.

2. Genetically modified food is nothing new.

In fact, we've been doing it for 10,000 years.
